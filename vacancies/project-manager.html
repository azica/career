Technical Project Manager

If your goal is to provide a qualitative project on time and exceed client expectations we would love to talk with you!
Who we are

DesigningIT is a US based full-service digital agency, that was founded in 2003 and is actively growing locations in Asia, Europe and the United States.

Our team focuses on performance marketing on a global scale. We help companies plan, build and grow their digital presence. Our core disciplines include digital marketing strategies. UX/UI solutions, modern front end code, CMS integration, and online marketing in the form of SEO and PPC.

Responsibilities
  • Use Clickup to create tasks and projects
  • Daily check in with designer, developer and client
  • Project estimation, requirement creation.
  • Make sure projects get done on time and on budget
  • Interface with clients to gather needed information
  • Work with designers and developers
  • Run weekly reports, monthly reports (time spend, hours worked, etc)
  • Solve blockers and issues
  • Testing and QA work
Required Skills
  • Experience taking a website design and development project through the entire lifecycle of the project from planning, estimation to implementation and launch
  • Programming background
  • Project Management background
  • Experience working in a agency, studio, or as a freelancer
  • Good written and verbal English skills
  • Technical experience
