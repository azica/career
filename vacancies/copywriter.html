Copywriter

If you are passionate about creating attention-grabbing, informative text, that describes and promotes products we would love to talk with you!
Who we are

DesigningIT is a US based full-service digital agency, that was founded in 2003 and is actively growing locations in Asia, Europe and the United States.

Our team focuses on performance marketing on a global scale. We help companies plan, build and grow their digital presence. Our core disciplines include digital marketing strategies. UX/UI solutions, modern front end code, CMS integration, and online marketing in the form of SEO and PPC.

Responsibilities
  • Write copy for a variety of channels including social media, blog and website
  • Edit and proof content to ensure high editorial standards are met across all content outputs
  • Collaborate with SEO and Marketing to create content strategies for our clients
  • Collaborate with our SEO team and marketing team to help with messaging, article idea creation, content creation
    • Work with designers, PMs, and marketing to develop content outlines, messaging and content for new websites
  • Execute the clients brand voice, tone, and messaging
  • Create quality and differentiated copy in the form of blogs, e-books, case studies, headlines, web copy, emails, social and more
  • Edit copy for style, grammar, logic, flow, repetition, and to ensure its on strategy
  • Conduct in-depth research to learn new subjects or industries as needed
Required Skills
  • Minimum 3 years professional copywriting experience with solid portfolio of work
  • Exceptional writing and research skill
  • Ability to work independently and with a team to meet deadlines
  • Excellent organizational skill and multitasking ability
  • Proficiency in Google drive, and Adobe Acrobat Pro
  • Social media or content marketing experience a plus
  • Experience working with content management systems, WordPress, etc.
